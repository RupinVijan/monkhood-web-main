Onbording

1. Booking will be considered only if 50% of one month's rent will be paid to Monkhood Living as a token amount. The token amount is subjected to be further managed into security deposits after deducting our service charges (i.e- 25% of one month's rent for students only ).

2. The token amount will be refunded if the booking is canceled by the tenants on or before 5 PM the next day. After this period, if the booking is canceled by the tenant then the token amount is subjected to forfeit.

3. If MonkHood Living declines the tenant’s booking then the full token amount will be refunded.

4. Only MonkHood Living has the right to confirm or decline any tenant’s booking. In case MonkHood Living declines any tenant’s booking, a notification via SMS or mail will be sent to the tenant on the same day.

5. In case any tenant uploads invalid documents, MonkHood Living can reject such invalid documents and extend the move-in date for resubmission of the valid documents by the tenant. Till such time, MonkHood Living will not cancel the booking and only move-in date will be extended.
